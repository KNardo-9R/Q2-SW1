An unfortunate boy with an unfortunate life granted a chance for happiness with the bestowal of his fairy godparents, Cosmo and Wanda. Timmy Turner, the cornerstone of Fairly Godparents.
  • Can summon his fairy grandparents at will
  • Can wish for absolutely anything he wants

The husband of Wanda, the male fairy godparent: Cosmo. He's simple and not very wise. He often leads Timmy into doing dumb things as a byproduct of his own knuckleheadedness, but at the end of it all---like most father figures---he makes Timmy happy.
  • Can summon anything for Timmy
  • Can be wiser than Wanda when the time calls for it

The wife of Cosmo, the female fairy godparent: Wanda. She acts as the voice of reason, her responsibility precedes none. She's reliable and a great component to Timmy's more rational decisions.
  • Can summon anything for Timmy
  • Has an insane love for sweets

Trixie Tang, the chicest girl in school. With all the fish in the sea, she's the girl that Timmy wants. She's pretty and rich albeit mean and sassy, and all eyes are always on her. Underneath the reputation, she has a warm heart.
  • Can make the entire school swoon
  • Hides a completely different personality underneath

Mr. Crocker, a horrible teacher who's license should be revoked. Unlike most adults, he believes that fairy godparents exist, and often harasses Timmy and others for this reason. He utilizes his intellect in the name of proving the tangibility of fairy godparents.
  • Incredibly smart for someone insane
  • Used to have fairy godparents too
